# Coffee Shop Encounters
## Overview
Coffee shops are one of the easiest, low-pressure environments to use your cúpla focal. Especially if you're there for a cup of tea!

This module can be taken by those who frequent coffee shops, or those employed in coffee shops. There will be coverage from both sides of the counter, but also the types of conversations you may have with whoever you're meeting and/or working with in the coffee shop.
