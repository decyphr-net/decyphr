# Vocabulary

We have looked at reusable structures so far. Use these accordion sections when you need quick word swaps.

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Drinks</summary>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Tae dubh</strong> (Teh duv): Black tea</li>
    <li><strong>Tae glas</strong> (Teh gloss): Green tea</li>
    <li><strong>Tae luibhe</strong> (Teh LIV-eh): Herbal tea</li>
    <li><strong>Tae le líomóid</strong> (Teh leh LEE-oh-mohd): Tea with lemon</li>
    <li><strong>Tae le bainne</strong> (Teh leh BAN-yuh): Tea with milk</li>
    <li><strong>Caife dubh</strong> (KAH-feh duv): Black coffee</li>
    <li><strong>Caife bán</strong> (KAH-feh bawn): White coffee</li>
    <li><strong>Meiriceánach</strong> (meh-rih-KAWN-akh): Americano</li>
    <li><strong>Caifé latte</strong> (kah-FAY LAH-teh): Latte</li>
    <li><strong>Cappuccino</strong> (kah-poo-CHEE-no): Cappuccino</li>
    <li><strong>Maicéató</strong> (mah-KAY-oh-toh): Macchiato</li>
    <li><strong>Mócha</strong> (MOH-khah): Mocha</li>
    <li><strong>Seacláid the</strong> (SHAK-lawd heh): Hot chocolate</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Milks and Alternatives</summary>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Bainne</strong> (BAN-yuh): Milk</li>
    <li><strong>Bainne leathbhearrtha</strong> (BAN-yuh lah-VYAR-huh): Semi-skimmed milk</li>
    <li><strong>Bainne lánbhearrtha</strong> (BAN-yuh lawn-VYAR-huh): Whole milk</li>
    <li><strong>Bainne coirce</strong> (BAN-yuh KIR-keh): Oat milk</li>
    <li><strong>Bainne soighe</strong> (BAN-yuh SOY-eh): Soy milk</li>
    <li><strong>Bainne almóinní</strong> (BAN-yuh al-MOAN-ee): Almond milk</li>
    <li><strong>Bainne cnó cócó</strong> (BAN-yuh knoh KOH-koh): Coconut milk</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Add-ons</summary>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Uachtar</strong> (OO-akh-tur): Cream</li>
    <li><strong>Gan uachtar</strong> (Gon OO-akh-tur): Without cream</li>
    <li><strong>Le huachtar</strong> (Leh HOO-akh-tur): With cream</li>
    <li><strong>Le leamhacháin</strong> (Leh LAV-uh-khawn): With marshmallows</li>
    <li><strong>Gan leamhacháin</strong> (Gon LAV-uh-khawn): Without marshmallows</li>
    <li><strong>Le siúcra</strong> (Leh SHOO-kruh): With sugar</li>
    <li><strong>Gan siúcra</strong> (Gon SHOO-kruh): Without sugar</li>
    <li><strong>Mil</strong> (Mil): Honey</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Dietary Options</summary>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Véagánach</strong> (VAY-gawn-akh): Vegan</li>
    <li><strong>Bainne véagánach</strong> (BAN-yuh VAY-gawn-akh): Vegan milk</li>
    <li><strong>Uachtar véagánach</strong> (OO-akh-tur VAY-gawn-akh): Vegan cream</li>
    <li><strong>Leamhacháin véagánacha</strong> (LAV-uh-khawn VAY-gawn-uh-khuh): Vegan marshmallows</li>
    <li><strong>Gan lachtós</strong> (Gon LAKH-tohs): Lactose-free</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Prices</summary>
  <p>Swap these into: <strong>Sin ___ euro.</strong></p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Dhá euro</strong> (Ghaw YUR-oh): €2</li>
    <li><strong>Dhá euro caoga</strong> (Ghaw YUR-oh KWAY-guh): €2.50</li>
    <li><strong>Trí euro</strong> (Tree YUR-oh): €3</li>
    <li><strong>Trí euro fiche</strong> (Tree YUR-oh FI-heh): €3.20</li>
    <li><strong>Trí euro caoga</strong> (Tree YUR-oh KWAY-guh): €3.50</li>
    <li><strong>Ceithre euro</strong> (KEH-hruh YUR-oh): €4</li>
    <li><strong>Ceithre euro caoga</strong> (KEH-hruh YUR-oh KWAY-guh): €4.50</li>
    <li><strong>Cúig euro</strong> (Koo-ig YUR-oh): €5</li>
  </ul>
  <p>For extra charges:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Deich cent breise</strong> (Jeh sent BRESH-eh): 10 cent extra</li>
    <li><strong>Fiche cent breise</strong> (FI-heh sent BRESH-eh): 20 cent extra</li>
    <li><strong>Caoga cent breise</strong> (KWAY-guh sent BRESH-eh): 50 cent extra</li>
    <li><strong>Euro breise</strong> (YUR-oh BRESH-eh): €1 extra</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Useful Swaps</summary>
  <p>Instead of: <strong>Cupán tae, le do thoil.</strong></p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Caifé latte, le bainne coirce, le do thoil.</strong> (kah-FAY LAH-teh, leh BAN-yuh KIR-keh, leh duh hull): Latte with oat milk, please.</li>
    <li><strong>Seacláid the, gan uachtar, le do thoil.</strong> (SHAK-lawd heh, gon OO-akh-tur, leh duh hull): Hot chocolate without cream, please.</li>
    <li><strong>Cappuccino, le bainne almóinní, le do thoil.</strong> (kah-poo-CHEE-no, leh BAN-yuh al-MOAN-ee, leh duh hull): Cappuccino with almond milk, please.</li>
    <li><strong>Tae glas, gan siúcra, le do thoil.</strong> (Teh gloss, gon SHOO-kruh, leh duh hull): Green tea without sugar, please.</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Clarification Questions (Barista)</summary>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>An bhfuil bainne coirce uait?</strong> (Un will BAN-yuh KIR-keh oo-it?): Would you like oat milk?</li>
    <li><strong>Le huachtar?</strong> (Leh HOO-akh-tur?): With cream?</li>
    <li><strong>Ar mhaith leat siúcra?</strong> (Er wah lyat SHOO-kruh?): Would you like sugar?</li>
    <li><strong>Rud ar bith eile?</strong> (Rud er bih EL-eh?): Anything else?</li>
    <li><strong>Beag nó mór?</strong> (Byug noh mohr?): Small or large?</li>
  </ul>
</details>
