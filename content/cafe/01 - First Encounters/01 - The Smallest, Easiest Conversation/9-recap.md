# Recap

OK, let's stop for a second and take stock of what we've covered here so far. In just one coffee shop scenario, you’ve covered:
- Greeting
- Ordering
- Payment
- Add-ons
- Extra charge
- Unavailability
- Alternatives
- Polite refusal

There's a lot here, and keep in mind that, much of what we've gone through here are actually set phrases that you'll be able to bring with you to a lot of similar situations, whether the setting is a coffee shop, restaurant, or shop, and you didn’t need complex grammar to do it!
