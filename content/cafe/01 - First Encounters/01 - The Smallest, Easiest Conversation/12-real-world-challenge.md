# Real World Challenge

Your challenge is simple:

Use one phrase from this unit in a real-world situation.

That’s it. One line.

Maybe start with something easy:

- "Go raibh maith agat."
- "Slán."

You don’t need a full conversation.
You don’t need perfect pronunciation.
You don’t need them to reply in Irish.

If they switch to English — fine.
If they hesitate — fine.
If they don’t catch it the first time — also fine.

You’ve still used it.

And that’s the point.

Most people are expecting English.
You’re helping change that — one small interaction at a time.

So next time you’re at the counter, don’t think about it too much.

Just say it.

The next coffee you buy is your first live test.
