# Bonus Order - Hot Chocolate

Right — time to put this together. No prompts. No subtitles. No safety net.

Walk into the coffee shop and order a hot chocolate, from start to finish, entirely in Irish.

Build the whole interaction:
Greeting → Order → Clarifications → Payment → Goodbye.

Out loud.

Do it from both perspectives, one where the customer requests various forms:
- With marshmallows, without cream
- With cream, without marshmallows

Do it twice:
1. Where the customer volunteers the add-ons.
2. Where the barista has to ask for the extra information.

If that felt comfortable, level it up:
- With a non-dairy milk
- With a non-dairy cream
- With vegan marshmallows

Add anything else you think might come up for a single-item order.

If you can do this, you’re ready for the real world.

Next lesson — you’re taking it outside.
