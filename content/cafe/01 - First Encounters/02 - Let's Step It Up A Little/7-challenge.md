# Challenge

Use the cards below to rebuild the full interaction with small changes.

Try four quick reps:
1. Swap one drink.
2. Swap one milk.
3. Swap one price.
4. Swap one closing phrase.

## Your Task

Rebuild the full interaction from greeting to collection using at least:
- one different drink
- one different milk
- one different price
- one different closing phrase

Keep everything else the same.
