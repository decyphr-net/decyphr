# Vocabulary

We've expanded the structure in this unit, so use these accordion sections for quick swaps.

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Sizes</summary>
  <p>Use these after: <strong>Beag nó mór?</strong></p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Beag</strong> (Byug): Small</li>
    <li><strong>Meánmhéid</strong> (MAYN-vayj): Medium</li>
    <li><strong>Mór</strong> (Moar): Large</li>
  </ul>
  <p>You can attach them directly to the drink:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Latte beag</strong> (LAH-teh byug): Small latte</li>
    <li><strong>Tae glas mór</strong> (Teh gloss moar): Large green tea</li>
    <li><strong>Cappuccino meánmhéid</strong> (Kap-uh-CHEE-noh MAYN-vayj): Medium cappuccino</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">For Here or Takeaway</summary>
  <p>Swap into:</p>
  <p><strong>Anseo nó le tabhairt leat?</strong><br/>An-SHO noh leh TOW-urt lyat?<br/>For here or takeaway?</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Anseo</strong> (An-SHO): For here</li>
    <li><strong>Le tabhairt liom</strong> (Leh TOW-urt lum): To take away (with me)</li>
    <li><strong>Le tabhairt leat</strong> (Leh TOW-urt lyat): To take away (with you)</li>
  </ul>
  <p>You can answer simply:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Anseo, le do thoil.</strong> (An-SHO, leh duh hull): For here, please.</li>
    <li><strong>Le tabhairt liom, le do thoil.</strong> (Leh TOW-urt lum, leh duh hull): To take away, please.</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Extra Items (Food)</summary>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Scónaí</strong> (SKO-nee): Scone</li>
    <li><strong>Ceapaire</strong> (KYAP-uh-ruh): Sandwich</li>
    <li><strong>Croissant</strong> (Kruh-SAWNT): Croissant</li>
    <li><strong>Briosca</strong> (BRIS-kuh): Biscuit</li>
    <li><strong>Fianán mór</strong> (FEE-uh-nawn moar): Large cookie</li>
  </ul>
  <p>Example:</p>
  <p><strong>Ó, agus scónaí freisin, le do thoil.</strong><br/>Oh, ah-gus SKO-nee fuh-RESH-in, leh duh hull.<br/>Oh, and a scone as well, please.</p>
</details>

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Late Add-Ons</summary>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Mil freisin</strong> (Mil fuh-RESH-in): Honey as well</li>
    <li><strong>Uachtar freisin</strong> (OO-akh-tur fuh-RESH-in): Cream as well</li>
    <li><strong>Bainne almóinní freisin</strong> (BAN-yuh al-MOAN-ee fuh-RESH-in): Almond milk as well</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Waiting & Collection</summary>
  <p>Swap into:</p>
  <p><strong>Beidh siad réidh i gceann cúpla nóiméad.</strong><br/>Bay shid ray i gyown KOO-pluh NOH-mid.<br/>They’ll be ready in a couple of minutes.</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Beidh sé réidh go luath.</strong> (Bay shay ray guh LOO-ah): It’ll be ready soon.</li>
    <li><strong>Nóiméad amháin.</strong> (NOH-mid uh-WAYN): One minute.</li>
    <li><strong>Tá sé beagnach réidh.</strong> (Taw shay byug-nukh ray): It’s almost ready.</li>
  </ul>
  <p>For identification:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Is é seo do chaife.</strong> (Iss ay sho duh KHAF-eh): This is your coffee.</li>
    <li><strong>Is é seo an cappuccino.</strong> (Iss ay sho un Kap-uh-CHEE-noh): This is the cappuccino.</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Updated Prices</summary>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Seacht euro fiche</strong> (Shakht YUR-oh FIH-heh): €7.20</li>
    <li><strong>Naoi euro fiche</strong> (Nee YUR-oh FIH-heh): €9.20</li>
    <li><strong>Deich euro</strong> (Jeh YUR-oh): €10</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Useful Structural Swaps</summary>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Dhá chaifé latte mhóra, le do thoil.</strong> (Ghaw Kaff-AY LAH-teh VOAR-uh, leh duh hull): Two large lattes, please.</li>
    <li><strong>Cappuccino mór agus mócha beag, le do thoil.</strong> (Kap-uh-CHEE-noh moar ah-gus MOH-khuh byug, leh duh hull): A large cappuccino and a small mocha, please.</li>
    <li><strong>Tae dubh mór agus latte meánmhéid, le do thoil.</strong> (Teh duv moar ah-gus LAH-teh MAYN-vayj, leh duh hull): A large black tea and a medium latte, please.</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details class="rounded-xl border border-slate-200 bg-slate-50/60 p-4 dark:border-slate-700 dark:bg-slate-900/50">
  <summary class="cursor-pointer font-semibold text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Extra Clarification Questions (Barista)</summary>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Beag, meánmhéid nó mór?</strong> (Byug, MAYN-vayj noh moar?): Small, medium or large?</li>
    <li><strong>Anseo nó le tabhairt leat?</strong> (An-SHO noh leh TOW-urt lyat?): For here or takeaway?</li>
    <li><strong>Rud ar bith eile inniu?</strong> (Rud er bih EL-eh in-yoo?): Anything else today?</li>
    <li><strong>Ar mhaith leat admháil?</strong> (Er wah lyat ow-VAWL?): Would you like a receipt?</li>
  </ul>
</details>
